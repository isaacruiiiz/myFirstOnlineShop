About Me

Hi there! My name is Isaac Ruiz and I'm an 18-year-old student currently studying DAM at Centre d'estudis politecnics.

I've always been fascinated by the power of design to transform how people experience and interact with the world around them. That's why I'm passionate about becoming a UI designer – I believe that good design can make a real difference in people's lives.

In my free time, I love exploring new design trends and experimenting with different tools and techniques. I also enjoy staying up to date with the latest developments in the tech industry, as I believe that technology and design are closely intertwined. As a budding UI designer, my goal is to create intuitive, user-friendly interfaces that make people's lives easier and more enjoyable. I'm excited to continue learning and growing in this field, and I can't wait to see where this journey takes me!
